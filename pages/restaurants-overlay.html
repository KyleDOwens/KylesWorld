Guide

This page holds all the restaurants in San Antonio that I have been to, or want to go to!

There is a Map View which shows the physical locations of each restaurant, and a Table View which lists out each restaurant individually.

Each marker on the map represents a restaurant. Clicking a marker will bring up its information, including a link to it on Google. Places I have visited are colored green. I give them ratings of how much I enjoyed it: low, meidum, or high. Places I want to go are colored blue. I give them ratings of how badly I want to go: low, meidum, or high.

You can click the checkbox in the table to show/hide a restaurant on the map. If you want to apply rules for which restaurants are shown, you can use the Filter Options window. This includes rules for visited/unvisted, cuisine type, and rating.

You can also add a Distance Filter to only show restaurants that are reachable from a starting point in a certain amount of time (fun fact, the map drawn by doing this is called an isochrone). To do this, (1) enter in the the max travel time in minutes. (2) click the Add Isochrone button (3) click on the map at your starting location.

If you click the Choose Random button, a random restaurant will be chosen from the restaurants shown on the map.

If you want to share your map with someone, click the Share Selections button! This will create a custom URL that leads to your exact map and automatically copy it to your clipboard, which you can then share.
