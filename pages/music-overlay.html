Guide

This is the page for me to share my music hobby! Each year I have the goal of listening to at least 100 new albums. At the end of the year I make lists of my favorite albums and songs I listened to, and put those here!

I don't like "out of 10" rating scores, so instead I use a rating system inspired by the olympic medals. I rate albums into different "tiers," where there is a noteworthy difference between the albums in different tiers. I use the colors to indicate these tiers, which is why you will see albums colored as gold, silver, and bronze. Each tier is also in order from favorite to least favorite, with the first album/song being my favorite.

(Also, every album listed here is something I really like, even if it appears "low" on my list)
